About

  • Being based on Tendermint / Cosmos SDK, Lendscape isn’t dependent upon the limitations related to the current ways to deal with decentralized lending.

  • Through ThorFi's hidden utility and mechanism, Lendscape enables fast and seamless borrowing-and-lending (ZIRP) alongside self-repaying loans.

  • By leveraging IBC (Inter-Blockchain Communication), Lendscape would be able to become the lending protocol for the whole Cosmos chain ecosystem. From THORChain to Secret network.

  • Lendscapers will be at the heart of the protocol and inherently define, shape, and control its future through Governance.
